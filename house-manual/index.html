About the Property

Fall asleep to the sound of the waves. This newly remodeled beach cottage is a 1-minute walk to a private neighborhood beach. Beach passes for 12 and a variety of beach gear are included. The cottage sleeps 8–10 with a king bed, kids' loft, two downstairs bedrooms, and a pull-out couch. Enjoy a living room that opens to the sun porch, games, AppleTV, a fully stocked kitchen, an extra fridge, and a washer/dryer! Enjoy the hot tub, outdoor shower, grill, dining area, and firepit.

Meet the Hosts

We're Kat, Adam & Jack

We recently bought and renovated this adorable beach cottage. We love spending time at the beach with our extended family and we've tried to equip the cottage for guests as if it was your own home.

We love to travel and always appreciate AirBnbs that understand just how much STUFF has to be packed for kids. We hope that we've provided some fun for the littles as well as the adults.
