INSTRUCTIONS

Arriving

Check In

  • There are two tandem parking spaces available in the driveway (which is on the right side of the grass when facing the house). Do not park on the street (prohibited by the town of Westerly) or on the grass or on either neighbor's driveways. This is the entire reason we have a doorbell camera as we love our neighbors and do not want to negatively affect our relationship with them!
  • On busy weekends, the main streets (Atlantic Ave. and Winapaug Ave) back up quite a bit. Turn onto Uzzi Ave from Shore Rd to avoid the beach parking traffic.
  • When you arrive, head in through the front door. The front sun-porch door will be unlocked. The main front door has an electronic keypad.
  • The key for the large folding door is on a bright blue keychain on the shelf by the bathroom door.
Departing

Check Out

  • We'd appreciate it if you put trash in the bins outside. You must use the orange bags for all trash or they won't take it.
  • Recycling can be in paper bags or loose in the bin.
  • Trash is picked up Wednesdays and Saturdays. The trash crew will come back to get the trash. You don't have to take it out to the curb.
  • That's it, we'll take care of the rest.
