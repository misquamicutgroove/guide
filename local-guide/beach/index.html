BEACH

The Misquamicut beaches are among the best in Rhode Island! All the gear and toys you need for a fun beach day can be found in the shed.

Misquamicut Fire District

Clark Farm Beach

MFD Beach Pass required. Closest beach to the house. The entrance is on the left side of the Pleasant View Inn parking lot. Has a lifeguard.

Benson Ave Beach

MFD Beach Pass required. To the left of The Andrea. A little bit bigger than Clark Farm. Has a lifeguard.

Misquamicut State Beach

MFD Beach Pass required. Very large beach past the Windjammer. Lifeguards and bathrooms.

Nearby

East Beach

Access it at the end of Atlantic Ave (turn right from Uzzi). No lifeguard on the part that's close to our house. It's a really nice walk all the way down to Watch Hill. If you want to play frisbee or beach games or play loud music, use this beach. The other ones get too crowded.

Westerly Town Beach

Huge beach north of the Misquamicut area.

Beach Pass Info

  • Two (2) beach passes are provided — each is good for 6 people. Look for the pink/purple lanyard.
  • Required at MFD beaches from Memorial Day to Labor Day during normal hours.
  • $50 fine for lost passes.
  • Beach rules, tide info & more: riparks.ri.gov/beaches/misquamicut-state-beach

What's in the Shed

  • Beach toys, mats, cooler chairs, umbrellas, beach cart
  • Two boogie boards
  • Please rinse sand off in the outdoor shower before coming inside or using the hot tub
