EXPLORE

South County is full of amazing towns brimming with history, great food, and fun kiddo activities.

Welcome to Westerly, Rhode Island! Whether you're here for a relaxing getaway, a family vacation, or a bit of adventure, you've chosen the perfect destination. Nestled along the scenic coastline, Westerly offers a unique mix of small-town charm, beautiful beaches, and vibrant culture. From historic landmarks to local eateries, and from outdoor activities to live entertainment, there's something here for everyone.

Westerly is known for its stunning coastline, with miles of sandy beaches perfect for sunbathing, swimming, or enjoying a peaceful walk. Misquamicut State Beach is one of the most popular spots, offering family-friendly amenities, beautiful ocean views, and seasonal events like outdoor concerts and drive-in movies.
